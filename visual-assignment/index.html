Mission Statement

The purpose of this site is to demonstrate several types of animal captivity through the use of photographs. Not all forms of captivity are fun to look at. There are cute pictures of dogs next to a video of baby chicks being shredded alive. If images such as these make you uncomfortable I highly encourage you to click through anyways. By doing this, you will gain perspective on how we humans treat our fellow animals.
