Treatment of Zoo Animals

Zoo animals are animals which were never domesticated but were instead caught in the wild and kept in cages for the entertainment of humans. I went to the Lincoln Park Zoo in order to take some pictures of these animals. Here is a picture of a gorilla sitting bored in a cage and an eagle sitting on the ground. These small boxes are clearly not the natural habitats of the animals.

Of course these wild animals have to be caught in order to be put on display. This can cause great distress to the animal. This is best shown by seeing the capturing of a wild animal in action. The documentary Blackfish provides a vivid account of a group orcas being rounded up and having their children taken from them.
